Famous Art Pieces & Artist

There are so many renowned artists, but we will only be mentioning three: Pablo Picasso, Leonardo da Vinci, and Vincent Van Gogh. The Old Guitarist was done by Pablo Picasso in 1903-1904, and it is oil on canvas. Of course the well-known Mona Lisa was done by Leonardo Da Vinci in 1503, and it is also oil on canvas. Starry Night is another well-known piece done by Vincent Van Gogh in 1889 and is also oil on canvas.
